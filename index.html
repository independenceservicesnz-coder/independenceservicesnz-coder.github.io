Who we help

Who Independence is for

Whether you're arranging support for yourself or for someone you care about, Independence makes the process simple and clear.

Older adults and independent clients

Ideal for people who want to stay at home while keeping support arrangements organised and easy to access.

  • Keep contact details and home address saved for future visits.
  • Review upcoming services from one simple dashboard.
  • See completed visits and leave service feedback.

Family members and care coordinators

Useful for families who want more visibility, cleaner communication, and easier planning around day-to-day support.

  • Store emergency and family contact information alongside the customer profile.
  • Track service requests, recent activity, and payment totals.
  • Maintain a clearer record of what has been booked and completed.
