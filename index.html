Our services

Practical help for everyday life at home

We focus on the everyday tasks that make a real difference — the ones that help you stay independent, comfortable, and safe in your own home.

🧹

House cleaning

Regular or one-off home cleaning by trustworthy local people who know what matters to older clients — reliability, care, and respect for your home.

Weekly clean Fortnightly Spring clean
🌿

Gardening & lawns

Lawn mowing, weeding, pruning, and general garden upkeep — so your outdoor space stays tidy and manageable without it becoming a burden.

Lawn mowing Weeding Pruning
🔧

Handyman & repairs

Minor repairs, odd jobs, light maintenance, and safety modifications — things like loose handrails, dripping taps, or replacing lightbulbs that are hard to reach.

Minor repairs Safety rails Odd jobs
🚗

Transport & companion driving

Safe, friendly transport to medical appointments, the supermarket, social outings, or wherever you need to go — with a driver who will walk you in if needed.

Medical appointments Shopping Social outings
🍲

Meal support

Help with preparing meals at home, or coordinating meal delivery — so that eating well stays easy and enjoyable even when cooking becomes more difficult.

Meal prep Delivery Grocery support
🐾

Pet assistance

We know how much pets mean to older people — the companionship, the routine, the joy. We coordinate trusted local helpers to keep your furry family members happy and well looked after.

Dog walking Pet grooming Vet transport Pet sitting
💪

Fitness, personal training & health

Staying active is one of the most powerful ways to maintain independence at home. We connect you with qualified trainers and health professionals who specialise in working with older adults — at your pace, in your own home.

Personal training Gentle exercise Balance & mobility Strength coaching Health check-ins

Why trust Independence

We earn trust before anyone comes through your door

Inviting someone into your home — or your parent's home — takes courage. We take that responsibility seriously.

🔍

Every provider is vetted

Before joining our network, all providers undergo a police check, reference check, and review of their reputation and experience working with older clients. We only work with people we would trust with our own family.

🏥

Trusted by local health professionals

GPs, physiotherapists, and community health workers across New Zealand refer their patients to us. That professional confidence is something we take seriously and work hard to maintain every day.

🤝

You are always in control

You choose which services you want and when. There are no lock-in contracts and no pressure. You can change, pause, or stop anything at any time — with no penalty and no complicated process.
